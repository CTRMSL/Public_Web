## About the design

VIGILO is positioned as a **Financial Intelligence Platform** for the three lines of defence — Statutory & Internal Auditors, CRO & Risk Management, CFOs & Executives.

Key features on the live page:
- 5-slide hero carousel
- 10 analytics packs · 1,050+ checks
- Plans · Engagement Tiers (Standard / Pro / Pro+)
- Full-population popviz signature animation
- BFSI use cases, Three Lines of Defence breakdown
- FAQs, Contact directory + Google Form modal
- Demo video walkthrough
- Founder profile: CA. Chandra Mohan
