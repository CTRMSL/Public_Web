Two Decades, Codified

From spreadsheets to automated pipelines.

The capability evolved with the tooling — without losing the auditor's judgement. Each stage compounds the last.

Stage I · Excel-based testing

Manual analytics in spreadsheets

Sample-based, slow, dependent on individual experience — the baseline most audits still operate at.

Stage II · ACL / IDEA

Dedicated audit-analytics software

Scripted checks, larger datasets, repeatable rules — a step-change, but still file-bound and largely engagement-specific.

Stage III · Low-code analytics platforms

Parameterised, sharable libraries

Logic reused across engagements; thresholds configurable per client; outputs more consistent across teams.

Stage IV · Code-integrated hybrid pipelines

Python-based automated analytics

Full-population testing, 10M+ records in <24 hours, >90% less audit-analytics time, defensible end-to-end audit trail.

Why we built VIGILO

Three problems professional audit has lived with for too long.

Problem 01

Manual testing doesn't scale

Sampling misses what matters. Population data is available — manual processes can't keep up with the volumes BFSI and large enterprises actually run.

Problem 02

High attrition in firms

Every cycle, the same logic is taught again to new joiners. Knowledge walks out the door. VIGILO captures the logic once and runs it every time.

Problem 03

Findings arrive too late

Risks don't wait for fieldwork. By the time exceptions surface, decisions have already been made. Exception-led analytics move the timing forward.
