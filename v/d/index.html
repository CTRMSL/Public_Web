Two Decades, Codified

From spreadsheets to automated pipelines.

The capability evolved with the tooling — without losing the auditor's judgement. Each stage compounds the last.

I.
Stage I · Spreadsheets

Excel-based testing

Sample-based, slow, dependent on individual experience — the baseline most audits still operate at.

II.
Stage II · ACL / IDEA

Dedicated audit analytics

Scripted checks, larger datasets, repeatable rules — a step-change, but still file-bound and engagement-specific.

III.
Stage III · Low-code

Analytics platforms

Parameterised, sharable libraries; logic reused across engagements; outputs consistent across teams.

IV.
Stage IV · Code-integrated

Python pipelines
