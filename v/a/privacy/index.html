1. Who we are

This website is operated by CTR Management Services (Pvt) Ltd ("CTR", "we", "us"), publisher of the VIGILO Financial Intelligence Platform. We are based in India.

2. What we collect from this website

  • Information you provide via the Demo Request form: organisation name, organisation type, your name, work email.
  • Email correspondence: any subsequent emails you send to us.
  • Standard server / CDN logs: IP address, timestamp, user-agent — held by our CDN provider (Cloudflare) for security and DDoS-protection purposes.

We do not set advertising cookies. We do not use third-party analytics on this site. We do not sell or share visitor data with marketers.

3. Why we collect it

  • To respond to your demo request, scope a pilot, and (with consent) follow up on future product news.
  • To maintain site availability and security.

4. Lawful basis

Where applicable: consent (you submit the demo form), legitimate interest (B2B outreach to organisations whose teams have expressed interest), and contract (when we deliver an engagement to you).

5. Retention

  • Demo Request form submissions: 24 months from last contact.
  • Email correspondence: 36 months from last interaction.
  • Audit-engagement client data: per the engagement contract and applicable audit-retention rules.
  • Server logs: 30 days (Cloudflare default).

6. Who we share it with (sub-processors)

  • Google Workspace (forms backend + email).
  • Cloudflare (DNS, CDN, DDoS protection).
  • The website host listed in our DNS records.

We do not share visitor data with any other third party for marketing or advertising.

7. Where data is held

Primarily in India and the United States (sub-processor locations). For audit engagements, client production data is held within the deployment model selected by the client — on-premise, private VPC, or our managed environment — as defined in the engagement contract.

8. Your rights

Under the DPDP Act 2023 (and GDPR / UK GDPR where applicable), you may request: access to data we hold about you, correction, deletion, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection to processing. Email info@ctrmsl.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

The site is served over HTTPS. Forms post over TLS. Production analytics deployments use the security model defined in the engagement contract (typically VPC isolation, role-based access control, full audit logging).

10. Children's data

This site is intended for business professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the latest version. Material changes will be summarised at the top.

12. Contact
