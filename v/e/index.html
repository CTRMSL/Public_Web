FAQs · Common Questions

Have specific questions? Browse our FAQs.

Short, practical answers to the questions audit, risk and finance leaders ask us most often.

Is VIGILO sampling or full-population testing?+
Full-population. 100% of records in scope — every transaction, not a statistical sample — run through 1,050+ automated checks. Only the exceptions are surfaced for review.
Does our data have to leave our environment?+
No. VIGILO supports on-premise and private-cloud (private-VPC) deployment. Analytics run inside your own information-security boundary.
Which systems can VIGILO read data from?+
Data exported from the systems you already run — core banking (Finacle, FLEXCUBE, BaNCS), ERP/finance (SAP, Oracle, Tally, Dynamics), lending and LMS platforms, the GSTN and TRACES portals, and flat files (Excel, CSV, database extracts).
How quickly do we see value?+
Engagements start with a bounded pilot on a defined scope of your own data. Once data is ingested, exception reports are typically available within 24 hours.
How is VIGILO different from ACL / IDEA / Excel?+
Full-population testing on 10M+ records, Python-integrated pipelines, over 90% fewer man-hours, exception-led outputs (not just data dumps), and versioned rule logic with a defensible audit trail.
Which regulatory frameworks does VIGILO map to?+
RBI Master Directions, IRAC norms, Ind AS 109 / IFRS 9, Basel III, KYC / AML / FATCA, IBC 2016, GST / TDS reconciliation, and Rule 11(g) audit-trail requirements under the Companies Act.
Is the output defensible for EQCR and regulator review?+
Yes. Every exception is traceable to source data, rule version, reviewer and supporting evidence — engineered for partner review, EQCR, TCWG reporting and regulator queries.
Is my data secure?+
On-prem / private-VPC deployment, role-based access and segregation of duties, full audit trail of every action, encryption in transit and at rest, source-to-output lineage, and rule versioning. Client-identifiable data does not leave your boundary.
Can we get a trial or pilot?+
Yes — a bounded pilot on a defined scope of your own data with a clear deliverable. Request a demo and we respond within one business day with a scoped pilot proposal.
What support do we get?+
Implementation support, methodology validation by a Chartered-Accountant review board, ongoing analytics support, and full documentation of rule libraries, thresholds and evidence packs.
