Dedicated audit-analytics software

Code-integrated hybrid pipelines

Why we built VIGILO

Three problems professional audit has lived with for too long.

Problem 01

Manual testing doesn't scale

Sampling misses what matters. Population data is available — manual processes cannot keep up with volumes BFSI and large enterprises actually run.

Problem 02

High attrition in firms

Every cycle, the same logic is taught again to new joiners. Knowledge walks out the door. VIGILO captures the logic once, runs it every time.

Problem 03

Findings arrive too late

Risks do not wait for fieldwork. By the time exceptions surface, decisions have already been made. Exception-led analytics move the timing forward.
